How do I Register for USAA Online and Mobile Access?


Registering for USAA online and mobile access is the first step to managing your finances, insurance, and other services from anywhere, anytime. Go to usaa.com/verify and select the registration option. You’ll be asked to confirm your USAA membership by providing your USAA number or Social Security number. After verifying your identity, you’ll set up a username, password, and security PIN. Once registration is complete, you can log in to USAA’s website or mobile app to access all your accounts and services securely.

Steps to Register for USAA Online and Mobile Access via usaa.com/verify

  • Visit usaa.com/verify in your web browser.
  • Click on “Register for online access.”
  • Enter your USAA number or Social Security number to verify your membership.
  • Follow the prompts to confirm your identity (you may need to answer security questions or provide additional details).
  • Create your username, password, and security PIN as instructed.
  • Review and accept the terms and conditions.
  • Complete the registration process and log in to your new USAA online account.
  • Once registered, you can use your credentials to access USAA’s online banking and mobile app features securely, helping you manage your accounts, insurance, and more.
